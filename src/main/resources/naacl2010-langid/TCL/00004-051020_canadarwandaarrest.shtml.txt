Polisi yo mu gihugu ca Canada ,iramaze guhagarika umugabo aba mu gisagara ca Toronto ,imurega ivyaha yakoreye ikiremwa muntu mu gihe c'itsembabwoko ryo mu miromgo icenda na kane .
Uwo mugabo Desire Munyaneza ,polisi yo muri Canada yatumenyesheje ko aregwa ivyaha bibiri vy'itsembabwoko n'ibindi vyaha bitatu bikoregwa ikiremwa muntu mu gihe c'ingwano .
Desire Munyaneza ,abaye umuntu wa mbere ahagaritswe hisunzwe itegeko ry'igihugu ca Canada rikurikira ivyaha mpanavyaha ,n'ivyaha vy'intambara .
Ivyo vyaha aregwa vyakozwe mu ntara ya Butare mu Rwanda mu gihe haba itsembabwoko ryo muri 94.
Igipolisi ca Canada cemeza ko urukiko rw'i Arusha n'ubushinjacaha bwo mu Rwanda babafashije mu kugira amaperereza yo kumenya idonido y'ivyo Desire aregwa.
