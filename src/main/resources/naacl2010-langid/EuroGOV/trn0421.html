Case T-115/04: Judgment of the Court of First Instance of 14 September 2006 — Laroche v Commission (Community officials — Career Development Review — Appraisal period 2001/2002 — Decision to close — Time-limit for challenge — Calculation)

Judgment of the Court of First Instance of 14 September 2006 — Laroche

v Commission

(Case T-115/04) [1]

Parties

Applicant: Yvonne Laroche (Brussels, Belgium) (represented by: G. Bounéou and F. Frabetti, lawyers)

Defendant: Commission of the European Communities (represented by: C. Berardis-Kayser and L. Lozano Palacios, Agents)

Re:

Application for annulment of the Commission decision of 11 June 2003 to close the applicant's career development report for the appraisal period 2001/2002.

Operative part of the judgment

The Court:

1. Annuls the Commission decision of 11 June 2003 to close the applicant's career development report for the appraisal period 2001/2002;

2. Orders the Commission to pay the costs.

[1] OJ C 106, 30.4.2004.
