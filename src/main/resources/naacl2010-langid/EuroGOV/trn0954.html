Decisions adopted by the Court of Justice at its general meeting on 16 May 2006

(2006/C 143/03)

At its meeting on 16 May 2006, the Court of Justice of the European Communities decided to assign Mr Tizzano to the Third and Sixth Chambers.

The composition of the Third and Sixth Chambers is accordingly as set out below.

Third Chamber

A. Rosas, President of the Chamber

J. Malenovský, J.-P. Puissochet, S. von Bahr, A. Tizzano, A. Borg Barthet, U. Lõhmus and A. Ó Caoimh.

Sixth Chamber

J. Malenovský, President of the Chamber

J.-P. Puissochet, S. von Bahr, A. Tizzano, A. Borg Barthet, U. Lõhmus and A. Ó Caoimh.
